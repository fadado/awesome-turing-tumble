## Challenge #19: Entanglement

### Objective

If the top bit AND the bottom bit start pointed to the right, intercept a blue ball. Otherwise, intercept a red ball.

### Setup 1

`balls:8B-8R; start:B; trace:b|rB`

### Solution 1

	|-.-.\.\.-./|           |-.-.\.\./.-|
